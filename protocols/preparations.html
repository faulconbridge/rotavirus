Preparations of Common Solutions¶

Preparation of Medium 199 (Serum-Free)¶

Items Needed:

  • Incomplete Medium 199 (\(500\)mL)
  • Penicillin/streptomycin stock (\(5\)mL)
  • Amphotericin B stock (\(1\)mL; \(250\mu\)g/mL)

In the biosafety cabinet, supplement \(500\)mL incomplete Medium 199 with \(5\)mL P/S stock and \(1\)mL amphotericin B stock. Store at \(4^{\circ}\)C for up to 3 months.

Preparation of Medium 199 (Complete)¶

Items Needed:

  • Incomplete Medium 199 (\(500\)mL)
  • Penicillin/streptomycin stock (\(5\)mL)
  • Amphotericin B stock (\(1\)mL; \(250\mu\)g/mL)
  • Fetal bovine serum (\(55\)mL)

In the biosafety cabinet, supplement \(500\)mL incomplete Medium 199 with \(5\)mL P/S stock, \(1\)mL amphotericin B stock, and \(55\)mL fetal bovine serum. Store at \(4^{\circ}\)C for up to 3 months.

Preparation of 1.2% Agarose¶

Items Needed:

  • SeaPlaque agarose
  • Milli-Q filtered water

To a \(500\)mL flask add \(1.2\)g agarose for every \(100\)mL water. (It is inadvisable to fill flask to more than \(400\)mL.) Cap tightly and shake. Loosen lid. Apply autoclave tape to lid. Autoclave for 20 minutes.

Preparation of \(2\times\) EMEM (Serum-Free)¶

Items Needed:

  • Incomplete \(2\times\) EMEM (\(500\)mL)
  • \(200\)mM L-glutamine (\(10\)mL)
  • Penicillin/streptomycin stock (\(10\)mL)
  • \(250\mu\)g/ml amphotericin B stock (\(1\)mL)

In the biosafety cabinet, to \(500\)mL incomplete \(2\times\) EMEM stock add \(10\)mL L-glutamine, \(10\)mL P/S stock, and \(1\)mL amphotericin B stock. Store at \(4^{\circ}\)C for up to 3 months.

Preparation of PBS (Phosphate Buffered Saline)¶

Items Needed:

  • \(10\times\) PBS (\(80\)mL)
  • milli-Q water (\(720\)mL)

To a large graduated cylinder add \(80\)mL 10x PBS solution (use a \(25\)mL pipet). Fill the graduated cylinder to \(800\)mL with milli-Q-filtered water. Apply autoclave tape to the lid. Autoclave for 30 minutes.
