Triple-Layered Structure of RV Particles¶

Rotaviruses are non-enveloped, icosahedral, triple-layered particles that encapsidate genomes consisting of 11 segments of dsRNA. The outer protein layer has \(T=13\) symmetry and is composed of 60 trimers of the viral attachment protein VP4 and of 260 trimers of the viral glycoprotein VP7.
